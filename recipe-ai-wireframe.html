🍗 Asian-Style Chicken & Bell Pepper Stir Fry

⏱️ Prep: 10 min 🔥 Cook: 15 min 🍽️ Serves: 4 🏃 Easy Level

🥕 Ingredients

  • 2 chicken breasts, sliced thin
  • 1 large onion, sliced
  • 3 cloves garlic, minced
  • 2 bell peppers, sliced
  • 2 cups cooked rice
  • 2 tbsp soy sauce
  • 1 tbsp vegetable oil
  • 1 tsp fresh ginger, minced
  • Salt and pepper to taste

👨‍🍳 Instructions

  1. Heat oil in a large pan or wok over medium-high heat
  2. Add chicken pieces and cook until golden brown, about 5-6 minutes
  3. Add onions and garlic, stir-fry for 2 minutes until fragrant
  4. Add bell peppers and cook for 3-4 minutes until tender-crisp
  5. Add soy sauce and ginger, stir well to combine
  6. Season with salt and pepper, serve hot over cooked rice
